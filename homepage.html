Welcome to the on-line home of Community Bible Chapel. We are a Christian assembly that meets every Sunday in a simple manner around the Lord's Table.
We are located in Hayward, CA on the east side of the San Francisco Bay Area. San Jose and San Francisco are both roughly 25 miles away from the chapel.

Sunday schedule:

9:30am to 10:45am - Breaking of Bread to remenber the Lord Jesus Christ as He requested  

10:45am - 11:15am - coffee time

11:15am - 12:30pm - Family Bible hour

5:30pm - 6:30pm - Bible Study/missionary report, usually followed by an evening meal                                                         

Mid-week schedule*:

Monday 7:00pm - Bible Study

Thursday 7:00 pm - Young Men's Bible Study

Friday 7:00 pm - Women's Bible Study

Saturday 7:30am - Prayer Meeting

*Some of the mid-week events happen at local homes. Please contact us for further information.
